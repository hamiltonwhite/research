Intro


This is a personal blog where I will be posting content relating to my Ph.D. I'm currently a Ph.D. student at WPI and the University of Massachusetts. My area of focus is in Concussion Neuroscience, studying the was neuromodulation can be achieved using mechanical strain, up to and including harmful levels. Posts to the left on the screen, like this one, will be shorter, and generally encompass short format articles about daily work, problems I'm encountering and what I'm up to. Posts to the right hand side will be long-form content about interesting research articles, talks I've been to and things that intrigue me. Obviously, expect the long-form content to be not as frequent. Anyways, welcome!
